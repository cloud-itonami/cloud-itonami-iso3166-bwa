# ADR-0001: BWA

Flagship `reservation-ineligible` (Public Procurement Act, 2021
s.76(1)/s.78(1) citizen-reservation ordered tier-classification),
conditional `tin-registration-unverified` (BURS), conditional
`bitc-facilitation-unacknowledged` (BITC/BOSSC, foreign-owned
engagements only).

**PPRA disambiguation**: "PPRA" independently names Kenya's and
Pakistan's own, unrelated, national procurement regulators. Every
"PPRA" reference in this repository means Botswana's own Public
Procurement Regulatory Authority, the s.6 continuation of the former
PPADB under the Public Procurement Act, 2021 (No. 24 of 2021) -- never
`ppra.co.bw` (unreachable), always `ipms.ppadb.co.bw` /
`botswanalaws.com` / Wayback-archived `ppadb.co.bw`. See
`marketentry.facts` namespace docstring and
`ppra-reference-disambiguated?`.
